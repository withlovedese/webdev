Books

I adore books. I remember the first time I actually “read” a book. By “read” I do not mean just looking at and comprehending the meaning of the words by mentally interpreting the characters of which it is composed. By "read" I mean jumping into the book. Being a part of the book. Understanding the words so much that you quite literally feel them. You feel the characters. You feel the setting. You feel the emotions felt by the characters deep in your bones. Yes, I remember the first time I “read” a book and it quite literally changed my life. I truly believe that they are a core part of who I am as an individual. My personality is the sum total of all the qualities I perceived to be the most striking of my best characters in the books I've read till date. I'm open to any genres but I really like the historical fiction, romance and fantasy subgenres. Here are a few of my favorite titles!
