Marius Von Hagen

Heir to a global corporation who yearns for freedom. As the heir of a conglomerate, everything Marius does is exposed by the paparazzi or media, resulting in few secrets and many headlines. Among the citizens of Stellis City, his reputation is surprisingly very high. But, high reputation is not always good as paparazzi often like to make explosive allegations. The media reports him as a carefree and rebellious playboy, and the outside world calls into question whether or not Marius has the ability to inherit such a large family business. The head of Pax Group and the von Hagen family does not comment, allowing public opinion and speculation to run rampant.
